B2B

The Bay Seaweed was founded by a group of seaweed enthusiasts who wanted to share their love of this nutrient-rich superfood with the world. We started small, sourcing seaweed from local fishermen and processing it by hand. Today, we have grown into a thriving seaweed export business, with a global network of suppliers and customers.
